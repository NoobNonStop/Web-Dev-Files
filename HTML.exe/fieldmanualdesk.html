Siddhant's Field Manual

My Mission

Dad retires in 2025. I am in my first year of college. After Dad's retirement, there won't be a bread-winner in our family unless I start earning. My mission is to develop skills of value in Web Development and Data Science, Machine Learning and Deep Learning before my dad's retirement so that I can support my family. I need to earn money since my sister is hell-bent on continuing to Higher studies, which I reckon would take more than 5 years. I have my education to pursue too. After I take care of this, I can continue on to fulfill my Vision.

What failure in my Mission means

Failure is something I cannot afford. Failure means I would be thinking of things on terms of money for some years, since I won't have a source of income. Failure means Poverty. Poverty for my family. Also, I don't want to eat away at my father's lifelong savings. My life would end up being another example of mediocrity.

Obstacles

Shows and Movies + YouTube

  1. Close the Apps in which you are consuming the Shows/Videos. Shut it DOWN.
  2. Review Mission statement and the consequences of Failure in the mission.

Books

In case it's an interesting book and you feel like procrastinating:

  1. STOP PROCRASTINATING.
  2. Schedule time-blocks specifically for books immediately and GET TO WORK.

Shit Happens

  • If possible, DELEGATE.
  • If unimportant, GET BACK TO WORK.
  • If not, and time taken < 2 min. on that task, do it now.
  • If time taken > 2 min. schedule time block for the task.
  • If Urgent + Important, do it NOW. Reschedule later.

General Rules to Live By

  1. Be consistent with Journaling. Do it atleast once everyday.
  2. Show appreciation for small things.
  3. Shows/ YouTube allowed only after 17:00.
  4. Maximize outputs in the Mornings.
  5. Read atleast 2 books per month.
  6. Finish all tasks for the day before going to bed.
  7. Carry out Weekly Reviews every Sunday.
  8. Disconnect atleast 2 hours before bedtime.
  9. Learn something New EveryDay.
  10. Surround yourself with positive people. Or, stay away from negative ones.
